Mantle RWA Guardian is an AI risk and yield copilot for Mantle wallets.

A wallet balance is not enough for real-world-asset and yield-bearing positions. Users need to understand concentration, risk, and whether the assessment can be verified.

In this demo, the app reads real Mantle Mainnet data for a wallet, detects tracked assets such as mETH, USDY, mUSD, and FBTC, and produces a clear risk score.

Then the AI agent writes a human-readable report explaining the portfolio, the main findings, and the risk context.

The important part is that the assessment is not just text on a website. Mantle RWA Guardian publishes portfolio and report hashes to a Mantle Sepolia proof contract. The result page gives copyable proof details: transaction hash, contract address, wallet address, portfolio hash, and report hash.

So the product flow is simple: real Mantle Mainnet portfolio data, explainable AI analysis, and a verifiable Mantle proof trail.

This MVP is focused on a clean hackathon demo, but the same pattern can grow into continuous wallet monitoring, alerts, historical risk charts, more Mantle assets, and deeper liquidity, oracle, bridge, and depeg risk modules.

Mantle RWA Guardian makes RWA portfolio risk readable for users and verifiable on-chain for everyone else.
